The Inn

Brava Club

Pousada Brava Club is a charming Inn with more than 10 years of market activity, located on the coast of the beautiful Brava Beach in Armação dos Búzios, just 2 hours from Rio de Janeiro and a 10-minute walk from Rua das Pedras and Orla Bardot, world famous and attractive spots for its variety of Shops, Bars and Restaurants. In addition to 29 suites, we offer an excellent infrastructure and an elegant and refined atmosphere. Complete comfort for your comfort and well-being in your Leisure and / or Business Tourism trips.

Our rooms are large and airy, and most have a lush view of the Atlantic Ocean.All rooms at the lodge feature cable TV, WiFi, hairdryer and safe.



Here we bring a new concept in local hospitality, where Brava Club accommodation and Silk Beach Clubs international restaurant lounge area are unified. This partnership also brings the best pre-programmed ballads in the city, from 4 pm to approximately 2 am, with famous DJS and residents (see agenda) and we also have special events (Business events, Weddings, Graduations and parties, see agenda) .

Following very used trends in the European coast, we are pioneers in this segment. Come and meet us and enjoy what was specially designed for you.
